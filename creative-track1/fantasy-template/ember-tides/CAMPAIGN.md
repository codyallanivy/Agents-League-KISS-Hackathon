# Ember Tides

## Premise
In the coastal town of Stormhaven, nestled between jagged cliffs and whispering seas, the air is thick with secrets and stormy veils. The tides have grown restless, whispering tales of old and foretelling a cataclysmic event known as the Ember Tides. This ancient phenomenon is said to bring both destruction and rebirth, but only the brave can unravel its mysteries.

## NPCs
- **Maren, Storm-Witch:** With hair like stormy waves and eyes that shimmer like lightning, Maren is a formidable presence in the town. She guards her younger brother’s secret with an iron will, believing it is the key to saving their home.
- **Odric, Tide-Priest:** A slender man with a calm demeanor, Odric dresses in tattered robes adorned with sea shells and fish bones. He speaks of the tides as living beings that can be heard if one listens closely enough. His true motive is to find a way to appease the ancient spirits before they unleash their wrath.
- **Galt, Exiled Cartographer:** A weather-beaten man with a scarred face and eyes that hold the map of a thousand seas. Galt’s secret is that he is feeding intelligence to an unknown entity, hoping for redemption through the secrets he holds.
- **Pell, Smuggler-Captain:** A charismatic figure with a thick beard and a twinkle in his eye, Pell commands a small but fearsome ship, the _Black Siren_. He collects maps of forbidden lands, believing they hold the key to untold wealth. His secret is that he is the vessel through which Galt's knowledge flows.

## Act 1: The Gathering Storm

### Overview
The party arrives in Stormhaven as a storm brews on the horizon. They are drawn to the town by rumors of missing charts and strange occurrences along the coast. As they explore, they meet various inhabitants who each hint at the growing danger, setting them on a path toward understanding the Ember Tides.

#### Act 1: The Gathering Storm

*“The tides have grown restless, whispering of the coming storm. A dark force stirs beneath the waves, and you are the only ones who can stop it.”*

**Scene 1: The Harbor**
- **Location:** Stormhaven Harbor
- **What Happens:** The party finds a smuggler ship docked, with signs of a recent struggle.
- **Skill/Combat Hint:** Use Perception to notice clues left behind by the attacker.

*“The _Black Siren_ is no ordinary vessel. Its crew was found dead, and something strange clings to its hull.”*

**Scene 2: The Cartographer’s Shop**
- **Location:** Galt's Shop
- **What Happens:** They meet Galt, who provides cryptic clues about missing charts.
- **Skill/Combat Hint:** Use Deception to convince Galt of their worthiness.

*“The maps are old and weathered, but they speak of places beyond the known world. It seems someone is gathering knowledge.”*

**Scene 3: The Tide-Priest’s Shrine**
- **Location:** Tide-Priest's Shrine
- **What Happens:** Odric speaks of the tides and their significance.
- **Skill/Combat Hint:** Use Arcana to interpret the priest's cryptic warnings.

*“The tides are alive, and they speak only to those who listen. The time for silence is over.”*

### Twist
Maren, who has been eavesdropping on the party from a hidden corner of the shrine, reveals that she possesses maps her brother drew during his travels. She believes these could be crucial in understanding the Ember Tides.

## Act 2: The Storm Within

### Overview
The party sets out to find Maren and uncover the truth behind the missing charts. They navigate treacherous waters and confront Galt, who reveals that he has been feeding information about their journey to a mysterious entity.

#### Act 2: The Storm Within

*“The secrets of the past are calling you. Only by confronting them can you hope to quell the storm within.”*

**Scene 1: Maren’s Hideout**
- **Location:** A hidden cave on the cliffs
- **What Happens:** They find Maren and her brother, who have been plotting a way out of Stormhaven.
- **Skill/Combat Hint:** Use Athletics to navigate the narrow pathways.

*“My brother was a cartographer, obsessed with uncovering forgotten lands. He left behind these maps as his final gift.”*

**Scene 2: The Smuggler’s Lair**
- **Location:** Pell’s Ship
- **What Happens:** They confront Pell and discover that he is the one feeding information to Galt.
- **Skill/Combat Hint:** Use Stealth to avoid detection.

*“The maps are just a tool. The true power lies in understanding what they reveal.”*

**Scene 3: Galt’s Revelation**
- **Location:** A hidden cove
- **What Happens:** Galt reveals that the entity he serves is an ancient spirit, the guardian of the Ember Tides.
- **Skill/Combat Hint:** Use Persuasion to gain his cooperation or prepare for a confrontation.

*“The spirit seeks balance. It will destroy Stormhaven if it deems imbalance too great.”*

### Twist
Maren’s brother has left behind a set of maps that reveal the location of an ancient artifact believed to control the tides. The party must retrieve this artifact before the Ember Tides unleash their wrath.

## Act 3: The Final Storm

### Overview
With the clues in hand, the party races against time to reach the artifact and prevent the impending disaster. They face a final confrontation with Galt and the spirit, and ultimately decide the fate of Stormhaven.

#### Act 3: The Final Storm

*“The time for action is now. Will you save your home or let it be lost?”*

**Scene 1: The Artifact’s Location**
- **Location:** An ancient temple on a distant island
- **What Happens:** They navigate the treacherous temple, facing traps and challenges.
- **Skill/Combat Hint:** Use Investigation to solve puzzles.

*“The path is fraught with danger. The spirit watches your every move.”*

**Scene 2: Confrontation with Galt**
- **Location:** Inside the temple
- **What Happens:** Galt appears, ready to use the artifact for his own gain.
- **Skill/Combat Hint:** Use Insight to understand Galt’s true intentions.

*“The spirit will take what it deems necessary. I can help you or hinder your path.”*

**Scene 3: The Final Decision**
- **Location:** The temple’s central chamber
- **What Happens:** They must choose between using the artifact for Stormhaven’s benefit or allowing Galt to take control.
- **Skill/Combat Hint:** Use Diplomacy to negotiate a peaceful resolution.

*“The spirit’s will is strong, but your actions hold the key. Will you save your home, or let it be destroyed?”*

### Three Endings

1. **The Guardian’s Mercy:**
   The party convinces Galt and the spirit of their intentions, using the artifact to restore balance to the tides. Stormhaven is saved, but at a cost as the town must learn to coexist with the ever-changing sea.

2. **The Sacrifice:**
   In a final act of heroism, the party uses the artifact, but in doing so, they realize its power comes at great personal cost. Maren’s brother sacrifices himself to ensure that Stormhaven can continue to thrive. The party mourns their loss but takes solace knowing they saved their home.

3. **The New Dawn:**
   With Galt’s betrayal exposed, the spirit is appeased by the combined efforts of the party and Stormhaven’s residents. They restore balance to the tides through communal effort, marking a new era for the coastal town where the brave can thrive alongside the ancient forces of nature.

The Ember Tides campaign comes to an end with the party's actions shaping the future of Stormhaven, leaving behind tales of bravery and sacrifice.

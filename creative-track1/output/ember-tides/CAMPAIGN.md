## Premise

Greyharbor has always lived by the tide-bells, but for nine nights the bells have rung backwards. Nets come up empty or come up *wrong*. The harbor folk whisper that the old compact with the deep is broken — and that a sea dragon has returned to collect. Into this heroic coastal town comes the party: not chosen ones, just the only people foolish enough to ask *why*.

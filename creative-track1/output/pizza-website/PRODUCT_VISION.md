# Product Vision — 'Pizza Website'

## Scope Split

### In Scope for Tier 1 (this build)
- Three-act outline for 3 sessions
- 4 named NPCs with hooks
- 1 cover art
- 4 character portraits
- 1 region map
- 1 session title-card
- Must-have: inviting nature

### In Scope for Tier 2 (after first session is played)
- Per-encounter art
- Alternate portrait outfits
- Animated/video intro
- Ambient music

### Out of Scope for Now
- Full illustrated rulebook
- Voice-acted scenes
